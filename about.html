About APS Agra Alumni Network

The APS Agra Alumni Network is a platform to reconnect graduates, support current students, and celebrate the spirit of our school motto—“Truth is God.”

Our Mission

Build lifelong connections, enable mentorship, share career opportunities, and foster a strong community that gives back to the school.

Why Join

• Stay updated on events and reunions.
• Post internships and jobs.
• Support fundraising and live initiatives.
• Be part of a vibrant global network.
